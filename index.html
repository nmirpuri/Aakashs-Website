Proven Wealth

Investment Analyst Intern

As an Investment Analyst Intern at Proven in Kingston, Jamaica, from July 2020 to September 2020, I conducted comprehensive research on blockchain technology. I focused on advanced concepts such as decentralized ledgers, smart contracts, and cryptographic security to support the development of innovative data systems. I successfully managed a $2M USD client portfolio, which included dividend growth stocks, ETFs, and bonds with varying ratings. These were carefully selected and tailored to meet the unique risk preferences of each client.

Utilizing the Bloomberg Terminal, I leveraged advanced financial analysis tools to monitor market trends, analyze key financial metrics such as price-earnings ratios, earnings per share (EPS), return on equity, and identify critical support and resistance lines. This rigorous analysis enabled me to make informed investment decisions and provide comprehensive portfolio reports.

Additionally, I deepened my understanding of the bond market by calculating durations and determining the most suitable bonds for each portfolio. This allowed me to effectively manage client portfolios and mitigate risks while optimizing returns. My role at Proven significantly enhanced my analytical skills and provided valuable insights into the dynamic field of investment management.

Digicel

Group Financial Planning and Analysis Intern

During my tenure as a Group Financial Planning and Analysis Intern at Digicel Headquarters in Kingston, Jamaica from July 2022 to September 2022, I undertook comprehensive financial data analysis, focusing on key performance indicators (KPIs) across various countries where Digicel provides telecommunication services. By comparing current data with previous years, I assessed whether performance improved, declined, or remained stable. My analysis significantly contributed to the preparation of the Q3 Quarterly Board Report and the Bond Report presentation for the CFO, which were subsequently presented to the Board of Directors.

Additionally, I conducted in-depth research on international cryptocurrency trends for the Legal Team. Utilizing PowerBI, I created interactive dashboards to highlight countries accepting cryptocurrency as a payment method and the percentage of transactions conducted in crypto. I also analyzed the evolving laws and regulations concerning cryptocurrency and projected future improvements in its acceptance.

Moreover, I developed a predictive model using R to forecast monthly subscriber counts across various national markets. The model incorporated variables such as previous subscriber counts, compounded annual growth rate, country-specific pricing, number of competitors, and network outages. With an accuracy rate of 95%, the model became a valuable tool for the Business Intelligence team.

In the realm of environmental sustainability, I played a pivotal role in creating the 2022 ESG Report. I focused on the environmental aspects of ESG, presenting to my manager the operational mechanisms and the Global Reporting Initiative (GRI) standards, specifically GRI 301 and GRI 302, that the company needed to meet. I outlined the company's current emissions and proposed reduction targets by 2050, emphasizing investments in renewable resources to achieve these goals.

Advisor Insights

Research Analyst

At Advisor Insights, a new startup firm in Toronto, Canada, I have been working as a Research Analyst since June 2024. My primary responsibility is to create insightful presentations for over 1,500 growing newsletter subscribers and investors, focusing on fundamental analysis and the current macroeconomic outlook. I conduct comprehensive market, financial, and economic trend analyses, utilizing advanced tools such as Excel, Stata, and Python for data mining and analysis. Excel is primarily used for charting and overlaying recessions, Stata for data cleansing processes, and Python for efficient data mining.

One of my significant projects involved completing a detailed report on the Big 6 Canadian banks, where I examined the effects of recessions, interest rates, and other macroeconomic factors on the banking sector. This project allowed me to improve my charting skills and attention to detail while analyzing financial statements to determine the investment potential of these banks.

I regularly collaborate with the CEO to ensure the accuracy of my work and receive valuable advice from colleagues, fostering a collaborative and supportive work environment. The engagement of our subscribers is evidenced by the growing number of subscriptions to our email newsletter and an increasing follower count on the company’s LinkedIn page.

One of the challenges I faced was navigating the learning curve of identifying which specific items on financial statements are most relevant depending on the company and industry. Overcoming this challenge has enhanced my ability to conduct targeted and effective analyses. Throughout my tenure, I have worked independently, consistently reporting to a manager for input and feedback, which has further refined my analytical skills and attention to detail.
